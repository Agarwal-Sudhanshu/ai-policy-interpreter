Policy interpretation should not be a bottleneck.

Lending policies evolve quickly. Translating them into operational, governed logic is where timelines and risk accumulate.

Manual, fragmented interpretation

Credit, policy, and product teams re-interpret the same PDFs and circulars across products, geographies, and channels—creating inconsistency and avoidable rework.

Ambiguity in implementation

The same clause is implemented differently by different squads and vendors, leading to variance in underwriting, exception handling, and customer outcomes.

Audit & compliance exposure

When rationale lives in spreadsheets and email chains, producing a defensible trail from policy to decision is slow, incomplete, and high-risk.

Slow launches & key-person risk

Product cycles wait on scarce policy SMEs. Knowledge concentrates with a few individuals, creating continuity and operational risk for the institution.

Product workflow: from document to decision API.

  1. 1. Upload policy PDF

    Add policies, addendums, and SOPs into a controlled workspace with access policies.

  2. 2. AI extracts clauses

    Identify clauses, definitions, thresholds, and exceptions and map them to standardized entities.

  3. 3. Convert into structured decision logic

    Generate rules, decision paths, and data requirements in a consistent, machine-readable format.

  4. 4. Human validation layer

    Risk and policy owners validate interpretations, resolve ambiguities, and approve releases.

  5. 5. Deploy via API

    Promote approved logic to dev, UAT, and production via API-ready outputs and versioned releases.
