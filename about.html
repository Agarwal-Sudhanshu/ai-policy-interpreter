About AI Policy Interpreter

AI Policy Interpreter is focused on a single problem for BFSI institutions: converting complex credit policies and risk frameworks into governed, deployable logic that can be understood by both machines and regulators.

The platform is built for banks, NBFCs, and fintech lenders who need to modernize underwriting and policy operations without compromising on governance, explainability, or security.
